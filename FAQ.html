Frequently Asked Questions

What do I use for college applications?

The Common, UC, and Coalition Apps are a few college applications that most students use. The Common Application is used by students seeking undergraduate admission to any of the 693 member colleges and universities in 48 states and the District of Columbia, as well as in Canada, China, and many European countries. The University of California (UC) Application is used by the nine UC schools: Berkeley, Los Angeles, Santa Barbara, San Diego, Davis, Irvine, Santa Cruz, Riverside, and Merced. The Coalition for Access, Affordability, and Success Application is similar to the Common App, but it allows students to start building a college portfolio online as soon as they enter high school. Additionally, the Coalition App represents more than 90 American higher education institutions.
